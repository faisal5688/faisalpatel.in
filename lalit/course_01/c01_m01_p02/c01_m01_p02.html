Performance: Speaking up about both concerns and ideas can lead to improvements in organizational performance. This happens in part because employees feel safer and more engaged at work.

Innovation: Employees who feel safer and more engaged at work contribute ideas and experience that can result in new and improved products, services, and business processes.

Risk: Individuals and the firm may be negatively impacted when concerns about unethical or illegal behaviors are not raised and resolved. We need to start from a commitment to do no harm, and that means speaking up about questions, concerns, or known problems to mitigate risk to you, your colleagues, and the firm.

This course will focus primarily on speaking up specifically to raise ethical concerns and address problems related to misconduct, values conflicts, or unethical behaviors. This is sometimes called "ethical voice."

In this course, we will primarily focus on speaking up about ethical concerns to directly address and resolve issues.
