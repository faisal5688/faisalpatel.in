Giving Voice to Values (GVV) is an evidence-based approach to ethics and integrity that builds ethical skills to speak up when it matters most.

GVV was developed by Mary C. Gentile, PhD, to build ethical muscle memory and a strength of habit so that you are ready to voice your values when it matters most. Mary is the creator and director of GVV, as well as a professor of practice at the University of Virginia Darden School of Business, senior adviser at the Aspen Institute Business and Society Program, and consultant on management education and leadership development.
