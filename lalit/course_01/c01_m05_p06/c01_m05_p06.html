Fudge factor in action: Conflicts of interest

Conflicts of interest arise when we have competing interests that could compel us to make an unethical decision in our own self-interest. We might, for example, fail to point out an appetizer the server forgot to add to our bill because it saves us a few dollars. Conflicts of interest can also be a problem in the workplace.
