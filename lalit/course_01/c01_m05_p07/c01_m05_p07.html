  • This course focuses on Step Four of the Ethical Decision Making Tool, Act with Integrity, in the firm’s Ethical Decision-making tool.

Am I ready to take action and speak up when faced with an ethical dilemma?

You should now be better able to:

  • Describe what a Speak Up culture is and recognize factors that can get in the way
  • Recognize that we often know the right course of action to take but can still struggle to resolve ethical dilemmas in a way that reflects our values
  • Recognize that values conflicts are a normal part of our personal and professional lives, and
  • Determine conditions that empower us to effectively voice our values and those that tend to inhibit that action.
