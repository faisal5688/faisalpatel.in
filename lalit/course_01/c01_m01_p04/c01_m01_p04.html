Pillar 1 Give clarity & set direction

Purpose

Our people lack clarity with, and connection to, the firm’s purpose.

We will: Clearly articulate KPMG’s purpose so that we drive clarity, consistency, and engagement in everyone’s work at the firm.

Values

Our Values are not fully understood in the context of the day-to-day.

We will: Integrate KPMG’s Values more clearly into our strategy and day-to-day decisions and actions.

Culture

Culture and Values are important to everyone and key to our long-term success.

We will: Establish a long-term sustainable culture to embed desired attributes in all aspects of our organization.

Pillar 2 Enhance Systems

Rewards

There is a perception that our Values do not inform performance and promotion discussions.

We will: Better align ethical and cultural expectations with assessment and rewards.

Compliance

Our response to issues is perceived as inconsistent and not transparent.

We will: Evolve our compliance program and strengthen transparency.

Governance

Our board can take a more active role across KPMG.

We will: Review the board’s role and expectations to take full advantage of the board and its expertise.

Pillar 3 Shift mindsets & behaviors

Candor

There is a reluctance to speak up, challenge, and provide divergent perspectives.

We will: Foster an environment where constructive challenge is highly valued.

Environment

KPMG’s high-pressure environment can create vulnerabilities.

We will: Equip our people to openly discuss and navigate pressures together.

Accountability

Leaders vary in the way they model our Values and can also be more accessible.

We will: Equip our leaders with clear Values-based standards and hold them accountable.
