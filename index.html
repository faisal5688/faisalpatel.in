About

A UX designer based in Mumbai, India. I have a passion for creating user experiences that are seamless and impactful.

I like to create designs that are genuinely desirable, feasible and viable for the business and the end-users. To make everything success, there are lot of experiments, requirement gathering from various stakeholders, prototypes and finally collaborating with the Engineering team. My experience span across user research, user personas, creating wireframes, interactive prototyping, creating impactful visual designs, mobile designs.

Understanding the human experience is important for creating useful and effective products. I use and try my skillset to empower people to accomplish their goals via easy to use design experience. Creating digital experiences that make life easier is what I love to do. ‍
